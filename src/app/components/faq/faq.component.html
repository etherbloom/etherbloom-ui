An Etherbloom is a generative fine-art NFT whose token hash is driven by a user-selected seed.  Each Etherbloom is made from a seed word or phrase that you enter when you mint your bloom.  That seed word or phrase then becomes both your NFTs identifier as well as its generative art seed.

A total of 500 Etherblooms will be minted. Each etherbloom is numbered to identify its place in the series, and each etherbloom is totally unique.

No. Once a word or phrase is minted into an Etherbloom, it becomes yours forever, and nobody else will be able to mint it. 

Etherbloom is a multinetwork ERC721 NFT and the tokensale will simultaneously operate live on Fantom, Avalanche, Polygon, POA, and Binance Smart Chain (the token itself will be multinetwork upon the release of my upconing NFT bridge technology). Tokens can be minted on any network, are globally unique across all bridged networks, and will operate seamlessly within the author's upcoming NFT multinetwork bridge infrastructure.

Etherbloom are only available to mint here at etherbloom.com and the entire minted registry is available for view on this site.

Each Etherbloom is an active collaboration between the artist and the minter.  Etherbloom NFTs are royalty-enabled, and Etherbloom minters receive 50% of the royalty fees collected upon the sale of their Etherbloom. Forever.

Each Etherbloom is also a key to a holders-only section where the artist will share content and art intended only for holders. Etherbloom holders become participants in an artistic journey through the heart of Sacred Geometry - the artist's favorite topic.

Etherbloom was created by Sebastian Schepis, a long-time member of the cryptocurrency community.  Sebastian, aka lonestar108, has been making digital generative art for years, and brings his twin passions - the beauty of sacred geometry and the potential of cryptocurrencies - together into Etherbloom.
